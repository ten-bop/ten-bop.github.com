Sprenger's Asparagus Fern (leaf)

[humerus] Scientific name: Asparagus Aethiopicus

Plant type: Herb

How they help us: Used for its ornamental beauty in gardens but also serves as emergency sustenance for wildlife during extreme droughts. Its strong root system stabilizes soil and prevents landslides.

How we can help them: Refrain from using nitrogen-rich fertilizers, water generously, pinch back stem tips for shape, and monitor for mites.
